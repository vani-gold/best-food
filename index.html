Cameroon Okra Soup

Okra soup is made with okra vegetables cooked in a delicious mixture of palm oil, shrimps, goat meat, fish and African spices and simmered to perfection. In Cameroon, okra soup is a delicacy and is popular amongst Doualas, Bayangui, Hausas, and other Cameroonian ethnic groups. In Doualas, it is referred to as "Obe Ila". I like this soup because it is very easy to prepare and likely the cheapest soup in Cameroon.

Okra Soup Ingredients
Ingredients Measurements
Fresh okra 25-30 pieces
Palm oil 1 cooking spoon
Assorted Meats/Beef 800 grams
Stock Fish 2-3 Pieces
Fresh/smoked prawns 50 grams
Chopped Scotch Bonnet peppers(Atarodo) 2-3
Locust Beans (Iru) 1/2 cooking spoon
Ground Crayfish Half cup
Knorr Chicken cubes 2
Salt to taste

Preparations:

1. Firstly, blend 1/3 of the Okra and chop the rest. If you want it chunky with no elasticity, you can chop the remaining, you can chop the, all or blend all if you dont fancy all chunkiness. Clean the smoked fish and soak in the hot water to remove all traces of dirt, shred and set aside.

2. Boil the meats on the medium to high heat with the chopped onion, Knorr Cubes, garlic and salt to taste. Start with the softer meats, add softer meats as you go. If you are using stock fish, add with the softer meats. You will need to season your meats realy well as this will serve as the base of your Soup. If your stock isnt well seasoned, then your soup may end up tasting bland. Ensure you end up with very rich and tasty stock

3. When the meats are soft, transfer the meats into a bowl, leaving just the stock in the pot. Add the palm oil and leave to boil on medium heat for 6-8 minutes... Now add the ground pepper, then the locust beans... NOW add the ground crayfish then the chopped peppers... Leave to cook for 3 minutes. Now add the blended okra. Stir vigorously to combine....

4. Now add the shredded smoked fish, prawns, cooked meats and smoked fish back into the pot. Stir and combine, then add the chopped okra... Add the fresh prawns if you are using anyy, leave for 5 minutes... And it is ready.

5. Serve with any swallow of your choice. you can serve with eba,fufu,pounded yam or semo.
